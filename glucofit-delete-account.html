Delete My Account

You have the right to delete your GlucoFit account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

Under UK GDPR you have the right to erasure of your personal data. Once submitted, we will permanently delete all data associated with your account within 30 days and send you an email confirmation.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

1
Open GlucoFit and go to Settings
2
Scroll down and tap Account
3
Tap Delete Account and confirm your choice
4
Your account and all data will be permanently deleted

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, email us directly. Please include the email address linked to your GlucoFit account so we can locate and delete your data.

Request deletion by email
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All glucose logs, nutrition entries, activity history, and personal settings will be erased and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your account details including name and email address
  • All blood glucose readings and logs
  • All nutrition and meal logs
  • All activity logs
  • Your personal settings and preferences

How long does it take?

We aim to process all deletion requests within 30 days of receipt, in line with our UK GDPR obligations. You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been fully deleted.

Questions?

Email us at adminglucofit@gmail.com and we will be happy to help.
