Frequently asked questions

How do I log a blood glucose reading? ▾
Open the app and tap the + button on the home screen. Select Glucose reading, enter your value in mmol/L, choose the time context such as fasting, before meal, or after meal, and tap Save. Your reading will appear in your daily log and history chart.
How do I log a meal or nutrition? ▾
Tap the + button and select Log meal. Enter the meal name and input carbohydrates, protein, fat, and total calories. GlucoFit tracks your daily macro totals against your personal targets.
How do I log physical activity? ▾
Tap the + button and select Log activity. Choose your activity type such as gym, walking, running, or cycling, enter the duration, and optionally add notes. Activity logs appear alongside your glucose data so you can see how exercise affects your readings.
What do my blood glucose readings mean? ▾
GlucoFit displays readings in mmol/L, the standard unit used in the UK. The app shows general reference ranges for context, but your personal target range should always be set in consultation with your doctor or diabetes care team. GlucoFit is a tracking tool, not a medical device.
Can I edit or delete a log entry? ▾
Yes. Go to your daily log, tap the entry you want to edit or delete, and select the appropriate option. Deleted entries cannot be recovered.
How do I delete my account and data? ▾
You can request account and data deletion at any time. Visit our Delete My Account page. Under UK GDPR we will process your request within 30 days and permanently delete all personal data.
Is GlucoFit compatible with my device? ▾
GlucoFit is available on iOS and Android. We recommend keeping your operating system updated for the best experience. Check the App Store or Google Play listing for minimum version requirements.
I found a bug. What should I do? ▾
Email us at adminglucofit@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and your operating system version. Screenshots are very helpful. We aim to investigate and respond within 48 hours.
How is my data stored and protected? ▾
Your data is stored securely and we comply with UK GDPR. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. For full details, read our Privacy Policy.
